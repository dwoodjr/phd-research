\subsubsection{Research-Creation and Practice-Based Knowledge}

\textbf{Narrative Contribution:} Loveless's \textit{How to Make Art at the End of the World} establishes research-creation as methodology that generates knowledge through making rather than simply applying existing knowledge to creative practice. Manning \& Massumi's \textit{Thought in the Act} provides theoretical framework for understanding how creative practice generates new concepts and ways of thinking. Bolt's \textit{Art Beyond Representation} demonstrates how material engagement creates knowledge that exceeds conceptual frameworks, supporting ST's emphasis on embodied knowledge generation.
